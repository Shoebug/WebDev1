Welcome to the Halloween Event guide. Below is a detailed guide on how to complete this event. However, if you would like the quick guide then click here.


Players must start by receiving a treat cauldron from the costumed children outside the Varrock west bank. The overall goal of the event is to collect treats by trick-or-treating around the kingdoms of Misthalin and Asgarnia.

Players can claim the Halloween rewards from previous years by speaking to the costumed children; players will need to wear three of said items to go trick-or-treating.

It is recommended to begin by trick-or-treating the following NPCs, each of whom gives a one-time reward of 100 treats:

Ned in Draynor Village
Elsie in Varrock
Iffie in Varrock
Town Crier in Falador
Lumbridge Guide in Lumbridge

Players can gain additional treats in multiple ways:
  • Using the trick-or-treat option on various NPCs around Gielinor (men, women, and guards). They will either give 3-5 treats, or trick the player, in which the following may occur:

    • Players are transformed into a spider, rat, bat, or costumed stray dog for a few seconds; players cannot control their character during this time. Having Protect from Magic active will prevent players from being transformed 85% of the time.
    • Players may receive a smelly sock, spooky egg, bruised banana, or an old wool. These can be combined into a terrifying charm, an ammo slot item that guarantees five treats for three uses.
    • Alternatively, a ghost may appear. Using a ghostspeak amulet to converse with the ghost will guarantee 5 treats for the next trick-or-treat as the player is temporarily granted a "ghostly presence".
    • The most recent 3 NPCs will ask to trick-or-treat other NPCs first before doing it on them.

  • Finding a Generous Treater, who gives 100 treats. Continuously logging out and back in will let you trick endlessly. Their locations are constantly changing, but are only in Varrock. The easiest way to find them is to hop worlds on one of the known spawn spots.
  • Mining (including crashed stars, but excluding Motherlode Mine), Woodcutting, and Fishing.




Rewards from previous events can be claimed from the costumed children upon starting the event.

Players gain new rewards for every 80 treats that are deposited into the great cauldron:

  • Cobweb cape
  • Spider hat
  • Trick emote
  • Halloween mask set x2
  • Pumpkin x2
