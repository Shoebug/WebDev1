Welcome to the Birthday Event guide. Below is a quick guide on how to complete this event. However, if you would like the detailed guide then click here.



Talk to Patty Seer in the Lumbridge Castle kitchen and receive and promissory note.

Talk to Bob at Bob's Brilliant Axes, give him the note. Return to Patty Seer and talk to her.

Talk to Warmhandz, found outside the Lumbridge General Store north of Lumbridge Castle.

Return to Lumbridge Castle and speak to Patty Seer again. Accept the teleport to the Falador party room.

At the Falador Party Room, talk to Party Pete, then speak with Archmage Sedridor, then back with Party Pete.

Check the gnome cake, the Varrock cake, the goblin cake, and the dwarf cake. Talk to Party Pete again when you've checked all the cakes.

When the cutscene concludes, speak to Party Pete again

Speak to Warmhandz (who is now in the party room with you) to tell them they've won, and are now one of the best chefs in Gielinor.

To finish the event speak to Party Pete. Once finished going through the dialogue you will receive your rewards.




  • 10th birthday balloons
  • 10th birthday cape
  • Bob the cat slippers/Jad slippers
  • Cake hat
  • Dragon candle dagger
  • Gnome child backpack
  • Gnome child plush/Jad plush/Stray dog plush
  • Oldschool jumper
  • Silver partyhat
  • Party emote
  • 2 noted Half full wine jugs
  • All previous birthday event rewards, claimable from Diango.
