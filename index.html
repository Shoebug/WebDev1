Welcome to the OSRS Event Wiki. This site will allow you to view all the events that happen throughout the year in runescape! You will never miss another event or struggle with completion with this guide. Offering you an indept detailed guide about the events but also a quick snappy quick guide for you to power through the events when they're live.


Old School Runescape is an online MMO that receives constant updates throughout its life cycle. Annually they perform special events corresponding with holidays in real life or their own community. Completing these awards can give you fun and interesting “Holiday Items”, cosmetic items that don't add any benefits to the game other than helping aid your “Fashion-scape”.

All the events are themed to its IRL counterpart, and the rewards too. Like silly birthday cake hats to wear.
