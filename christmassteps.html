Welcome to the Christmas Event guide. Below is a quick guide on how to complete this event. However, if you would like the detailed guide then click here.



Talk to Jack Frost to start the event and enter the pen.

Complete the set of challenges by clicking directly on the beacons in the pen. Returning to Jack after each completion.

When Anti-Santa appears, collect snowballs, equip them, and enter the gate and pelt Anti-Santa. Hit him 10 tens and click through the dialogue each time.

Once he says "Ow! I get the idea, you can stop now," click the dialogue and then click on Anti-Santa.

Pet the wild goose, east of him.

Return to Jack to finish the event.




  • Snowglobe helmet
  • Icy jumper
  • Two noted partyhat sets
  • Two noted Santa hats
  • Two noted Christmas crackers
  • Rewards from previous events, which can be claimed from Diango.
