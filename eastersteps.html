Welcome to the Easter Event guide. Below is a quick guide on how to complete this event. However, if you would like the detailed guide then click here.



Talk to the Easter Bunny east of Varrock's west bank and agree to help him.

Head west and speak to Gertrude, she tells you about her children and to speak to them.

Talk to Philop, then talk to Kanel and help him with his homework. Input "HAPPY EASTER" and he will tell you about Wilough and Shilop

Go to the Varrock Sqaure and speak to them. Input "drain" as the answer.

Play the game with Wilough, then talk with Horvik. Claim the item he gives you and use it against Wilough.

Return to the Easter Bunny and collect the Eastfloor spade.

Head to the east side of the Blue Moon Inn, and dig on the tile to the east of the drain to retrieve the egg mould.

Return to the Easter Bunny, right-cick "Get-ingredients" to obtain chocolate dust. Repeat this until you have ten dust.

1. Dust the egg mould with Easter chocolate dust to obtain a dusted egg mould.
2. Use the dusted mould on a fire/range to obtain a hot egg mould.
3. Empty the hot mould to obtain an Easter egg half. Make another one.
4. Combine two halves to obtain a warm easter egg.
5. Use the warm easter egg on the bucket of freezing milk to obtain an Easter egg. Make four more.

Talk to the Easter Bunny after making five eggs, then talk to Gertrude and give her the eggs.

Return to the Easter Bunny to complete the event.




After completing this event players will receive:

  • Eastfloor spade
  • Giant bronze dager
  • Nest hat
  • Two noted Easter eggs
  • All other Easter event rewards from previous years, which can also be (re)obtained from Diango
